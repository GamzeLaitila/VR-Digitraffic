This Single Page Application was made to visualize specific train actions, depending on GPS information and date, chosen by the user

 

  • 'Map' section allows the user to pick a spot on the map and brings the data on the screen from the last 15 minutes. Data involves information - about the trains within 100 km of radius of the chosen spot - which are trains' identification numbers and locations at the time

  • 'Calendar' section allows the user to pick a day from the calendar and brings a list of the trains that were on the move on the chosen day. User then can choose a train and view the departure/arrival train stations and times of the chosen train
